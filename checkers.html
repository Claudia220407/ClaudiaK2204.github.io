Welcome to Checkers!

Rules:

  • Player 1 starts the game

  • You can only move the piece when the tiles are highlighted green, if none are highlighted it means there is no valid move possible

  • Everytime you grab an opponents piece, you get a point

  • If you reach the other end of the board, it means you have become king and that piece will also become yellow.

  • the game ends when you or your opponents have no pieces left.

  • Have fun!!
